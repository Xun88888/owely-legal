The one-line version: Owely collects no data about you. Everything you record stays on your own device.

What we collect

Nothing. Owely has no server, no accounts, no analytics, and no ad SDKs. Your contacts, transactions, and receipt photos are stored only in the local database on your device and are never sent to us or to any third party.

Photos, Calendar, and Face ID

The app requests Photo Library access (to attach receipts), Calendar access (to create due-date reminders), and Face ID (for the app lock) only when you actively use those features. Everything runs on-device; receipt amount recognition uses Apple's on-device text recognition, and images never leave your device.

Purchases

Pro is a one-time purchase handled entirely by the Apple App Store. We never see your payment information.

Deleting your data

Deleting the app deletes all of your data. We hold no copies.
