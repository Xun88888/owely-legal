Rollog 刷街日記 — 隱私權政策

最後更新:2026 年 7 月 17 日

Rollog 不收集、不儲存、也不上傳任何個人資料到任何伺服器。本 App 為單機(Local-First)設計,沒有帳號系統、沒有自家後端伺服器。

1. 資料儲存

你的滑行紀錄(GPS 軌跡、距離、速度、熱量)、滑行日記、照片標記、裝備資料與個人設定(年齡、體重、性別)全部只儲存在你的裝置本機,解除安裝 App 即隨之刪除。

2. 定位權限

Rollog 需要定位權限以記錄滑行軌跡、距離與速度。定位資料只在你的裝置上處理與儲存,不會傳送給我們或任何第三方。分享路線(GPX)時,預設會自動裁剪路線頭尾各 200 公尺以保護你的住家位置,且分享與否完全由你決定。

3. 相機權限

拍攝路況或風景照片並標記在滑行地圖上。照片只存在本機,不會上傳。

4. 健康資料(Apple 健康)

若你使用 Apple Watch 並授權,Rollog 會讀取心率以計算燃脂區間與更準確的熱量,並可將滑行儲存為 Apple 健康的體能訓練。健康資料的存取完全由 Apple HealthKit 框架管理,只在裝置上處理,我們無法取得。

5. 天氣資訊

滑行結束時透過 Apple WeatherKit 取得當下天氣快照(氣溫、風速、天況)。此請求由 Apple 處理,受 Apple 隱私權政策約束,我們不會取得你的位置。

6. App 內購買

Rollog Pro 為一次性買斷,交易完全由 Apple App Store 處理,我們不會取得你的付款資訊。

7. 第三方服務

Rollog 不整合任何第三方分析、廣告或追蹤 SDK。僅使用 Apple 第一方框架(MapKit、WeatherKit、HealthKit、StoreKit)。

8. 兒童隱私

Rollog 不收集任何人(包含兒童)的個人資料。

9. 政策變更

若本政策有重大變更,我們會在此頁面更新並修改「最後更新」日期。

10. 聯絡我們

對本政策有任何疑問,歡迎來信:xunstudio.support@gmail.com

Rollog — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Rollog does not collect, store, or upload any personal data to any server. The app is local-first: there is no account system and no backend server of ours.

1. Data storage

Your skating sessions (GPS routes, distance, speed, calories), diary entries, photo marks, gear data and personal settings (age, weight, sex) are stored only on your device and are deleted when you uninstall the app.

2. Location permission

Rollog needs location access to record your skating route, distance and speed. Location data is processed and stored on-device only and is never sent to us or any third party. When sharing a route (GPX), the first and last 200 m are trimmed by default to protect your home location, and sharing is always your choice.

3. Camera permission

Used to photograph road conditions or scenery and pin them on your skating map. Photos stay on your device.

4. Health data (Apple Health)

If you use an Apple Watch and grant permission, Rollog reads your heart rate to calculate fat-burn zones and more accurate calories, and can save sessions as workouts in Apple Health. Health data access is managed entirely by Apple's HealthKit framework and processed on-device; we never receive it.

5. Weather

When a session ends, a weather snapshot (temperature, wind, condition) is fetched via Apple WeatherKit. This request is handled by Apple under the Apple Privacy Policy; we do not receive your location.

6. In-app purchase

Rollog Pro is a one-time purchase processed entirely by the Apple App Store. We never receive your payment information.

7. Third-party services

Rollog integrates no third-party analytics, advertising or tracking SDKs. Only Apple first-party frameworks (MapKit, WeatherKit, HealthKit, StoreKit) are used.

8. Children's privacy

Rollog does not collect personal data from anyone, including children.

9. Changes to this policy

Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

Questions about this policy: xunstudio.support@gmail.com
